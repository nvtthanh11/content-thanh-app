1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Content_thanh ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when a creator uses the App to manage and upload video content to their own TikTok account. By connecting your TikTok account to the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • TikTok profile information (via Login Kit): your TikTok open ID, display name, and avatar, obtained only after you sign in and authorize the App through TikTok's official login flow.
  • Video content you choose to upload: the video files you select inside the App for the purpose of uploading them to your TikTok account as drafts, via the video.upload scope of the Content Posting API.
  • Basic technical information: limited device and log information (such as app version and error logs) used to keep the App working reliably.

We do not collect your TikTok password. Authentication happens entirely through TikTok's official Login Kit, and we never see or store your TikTok credentials.

3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate you and confirm which TikTok account is connected to the App.
  • To upload the video content you select to your authorized TikTok account as a draft.
  • To display your basic profile details (name, avatar) inside the App so you can confirm you're signed in to the correct account.
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues with the App.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on your consent, given when you authorize the App through TikTok Login Kit, and to the extent necessary to perform the video-upload service you request.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data with TikTok, and only as required to perform the actions you request (such as uploading a video draft to your account) through TikTok's official APIs. We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your TikTok profile information for as long as your account remains connected to the App. Video files you upload through the App are held only as long as needed to complete the upload to TikTok, after which local copies used for that upload are deleted. You may request deletion of any remaining data at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including limiting access to authorized processes only.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may disconnect the App from your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok account settings, which immediately revokes our access to your data.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you by contacting us using the details below.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children and is not intended for use by anyone who does not meet TikTok's own minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Information may be processed in a country other than the one in which you reside. Where this occurs, we take steps to ensure your information continues to receive an appropriate level of protection.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App or applicable law. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes are made. Continued use of the App after changes take effect means you accept the revised Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any of the rights described above, please contact us at nvtthanh11@gmail.com.
