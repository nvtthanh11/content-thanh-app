About Content_Thanh

Who this is for and who built it.

Content_Thanh is a small personal utility built and maintained by an individual developer (Nguyễn Vũ Trường Thanh) for managing my own TikTok posting workflow. It is not a public SaaS product, and it is not offered as a service to third parties — it connects to a single TikTok account that I own and authorize myself through TikTok's official Login Kit. If you have any questions about how it works or how your data is handled, reach out any time at nvtthanh11@gmail.com.

What Content_Thanh does

A lightweight companion tool for a single creator's own posting workflow — not a public app or marketplace.

Organize videos in one place

Keeps a queue of the video files a creator wants to publish, so they don't have to dig through folders when it's time to post.

Upload to TikTok as drafts

Sends selected videos to the creator's own authorized TikTok account as drafts via the Content Posting API, ready for a final review before they go live.

Secure TikTok sign-in

Uses TikTok's official Login Kit for authentication — Content_Thanh never sees or stores the creator's TikTok password.

Basic profile display

Shows the connected creator's display name and avatar inside the app so they can confirm they're posting from the right account.

How it works

End-to-end flow using TikTok for Developers' Login Kit and Content Posting API.

1

Connect your TikTok account

The creator signs in with TikTok Login Kit and authorizes Content_Thanh to access basic profile info.

2

Pick a video to publish

The creator selects a video file from their own library inside Content_Thanh.

3

Upload as a draft

Content_Thanh calls the Content Posting API to upload the video to TikTok as a draft, which the creator then reviews and posts from the TikTok app.

Permissions Content_Thanh requests

Only the scopes required for the flow above — nothing more.

user.info.basic
Basic profile information

Reads the connected creator's open ID, display name, and avatar so Content_Thanh can confirm which account is signed in.

video.upload
Upload video as draft

Uploads a selected video to the creator's TikTok account as a draft, for the creator to edit and publish themselves inside TikTok.

Frequently asked questions

Common questions about how Content_Thanh works and how your data is handled.

Why does Content_Thanh need TikTok login access?

Content_Thanh uses TikTok's official Login Kit (user.info.basic) only to confirm which TikTok account is currently connected, so the right account's display name and avatar show inside the App before you upload anything.

Why does Content_Thanh need the video.upload scope?

This is the only way to send a video from the App to your TikTok account through TikTok's Content Posting API. The video is uploaded as a draft — TikTok requires you to open the TikTok app and manually review and publish it yourself; Content_Thanh cannot publish on your behalf.

Is my video content or account data shared with anyone else?

No. Data is only exchanged directly with TikTok's official APIs to perform the upload you request. Content_Thanh does not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I revoke access at any time?

Yes. You can disconnect Content_Thanh from your TikTok account at any time from your TikTok account settings, which immediately revokes all access.

Is Content_Thanh a public product other people can sign up for?

No. Content_Thanh is a personal utility built for a single developer's own TikTok posting workflow, not a commercial service offered to the public.
